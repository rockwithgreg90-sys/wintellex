Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Wintellex may collect, use, store, and protect information related to visitors, users, organizations, and operational workflows associated with the platform.

Last Updated • May 2026

1. Introduction

Wintellex is focused on providing operational finance infrastructure, workflow systems, payment coordination concepts, treasury visibility tools, settlement workflows, and related services.

By accessing or using Wintellex websites, applications, infrastructure, APIs, or related services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We May Collect

Wintellex may collect information including:

  • Name, email address, and organization details
  • Account credentials and login activity
  • Operational workflow information
  • Invoice, payment, and transaction-related records
  • API requests and developer activity
  • Device, browser, IP address, and usage analytics
  • Support requests and communication records
Operational Data

Organizations using Wintellex may store operational workflow information including invoices, payment records, contracts, approvals, client records, settlement data, treasury activity, and internal business documentation.

3. How Information May Be Used

Wintellex may use information for purposes including:

  • Providing and improving platform functionality
  • Supporting operational workflows and reporting
  • Maintaining security and fraud prevention processes
  • Managing developer integrations and APIs
  • Supporting customer service and communications
  • Monitoring platform performance and infrastructure stability
  • Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and requests

4. Security & Protection

Wintellex uses administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to support platform security, operational governance, and access management.

Security measures may include:

  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Operational monitoring and audit visibility
  • Role-based permissions
  • Infrastructure review processes
  • Internal governance procedures

No method of electronic transmission or storage is guaranteed to be fully secure. Users remain responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their credentials and internal systems.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Wintellex may use cookies, analytics tools, and related technologies to:

  • Improve platform functionality
  • Measure usage and performance
  • Maintain sessions and preferences
  • Support operational monitoring
  • Improve infrastructure reliability

Users may adjust browser settings to limit or disable cookies where supported.

6. Third-Party Services

Wintellex may integrate with or reference third-party infrastructure providers, analytics tools, APIs, payment providers, hosting services, developer tools, and operational platforms.

Third-party services operate under their own policies and practices. Wintellex is not responsible for third-party websites, systems, products, or services.

7. Data Retention

Information may be retained for operational, legal, security, compliance, reporting, or infrastructure purposes for periods determined appropriate by Wintellex.

Retention periods may vary depending on:

  • Operational requirements
  • Security reviews
  • Platform activity
  • Regulatory considerations
  • Internal governance procedures

8. User Responsibilities

Organizations and users are responsible for:

  • Maintaining secure credentials
  • Managing authorized user access
  • Reviewing operational workflows
  • Using infrastructure in accordance with applicable laws
  • Protecting sensitive business information

9. Policy Updates

Wintellex may update or modify this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates become effective upon publication unless otherwise stated.

Continued use of Wintellex services following updates constitutes acceptance of revised terms.

10. Contact

Questions regarding this Privacy Policy or Wintellex operational practices may be directed through the official Wintellex contact channels.
