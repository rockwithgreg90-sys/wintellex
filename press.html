Wintellex raises $1.2B Series F at a $20B valuation

The round will accelerate expansion of Wintellex's global payment infrastructure, treasury coordination platform, and enterprise workflow systems. The financing was led by Starlite Capital Partners with participation from Fenelon Opportunity Fund and existing backers.

Wintellex, the financial infrastructure platform connecting payments, treasury, and enterprise workflows, today announced it has raised $1.2 billion in a Series F funding round at a $20 billion post-money valuation.

The capital will be used to scale global settlement capabilities, deepen AI-driven compliance and fraud prevention tools, and expand its enterprise and government client base. Wintellex has experienced rapid adoption as organizations consolidate mission-critical financial operations onto a single infrastructure layer.

"This round is validation that enterprises are ready for connected financial infrastructure. We're building the operating system for modern treasury and payments at global scale."

Gregory Fenelon, Founder & CEO, Wintellex

Use of Proceeds

  • Expansion of engineering and infrastructure teams focused on global settlement and treasury coordination.
  • International market expansion into Europe and Asia-Pacific.
  • Advancement of AI compliance, risk intelligence, and fraud prevention capabilities.
  • Growth of enterprise sales, government partnerships, and go-to-market teams.
