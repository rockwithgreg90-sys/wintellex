Modern Slavery Statement

Wintellex is committed to ethical operations, responsible business conduct, and preventing modern slavery, forced labor, and human trafficking within our business and supply chain.

Last Updated • May 2026

1. Our Commitment

Wintellex opposes all forms of modern slavery, forced labor, child labor, debt bondage, involuntary servitude, and human trafficking.

We are committed to operating with integrity and expect our employees, contractors, suppliers, service providers, technology partners, and business relationships to uphold the same standards.

2. Business Overview

Wintellex provides intelligent payment infrastructure, operational finance systems, workflow tools, treasury visibility concepts, developer systems, and enterprise-oriented infrastructure services.

Our operations are primarily technology, software, infrastructure, and professional-service focused. We recognize that risks may still exist across third-party vendors, outsourced services, contractors, data providers, hosting providers, facilities, and business relationships.

3. Supply Chain Expectations

Wintellex expects suppliers and service providers to:

  • Comply with applicable labor, employment, and human rights laws
  • Prohibit forced labor, bonded labor, child labor, and human trafficking
  • Provide safe and respectful working conditions
  • Maintain lawful employment practices
  • Respect worker dignity, compensation, and freedom from coercion
  • Cooperate with reasonable diligence, review, or information requests

4. Risk Management

Wintellex seeks to identify and reduce modern slavery risks through reasonable business review processes, vendor assessment, contractual expectations, internal governance, and responsible procurement practices.

Risk Areas Reviewed

Wintellex may review vendors, contractors, outsourced service providers, infrastructure partners, staffing relationships, facility services, professional service firms, and technology suppliers for potential operational or ethical risk.

5. Policies & Controls

Wintellex maintains a governance-oriented approach to ethical business conduct. Internal expectations may include:

  • Responsible vendor selection and review
  • Contractual expectations for lawful labor practices
  • Escalation of concerns involving worker exploitation
  • Review of high-risk supplier relationships
  • Compliance-oriented internal decision making
  • Ongoing improvement of governance processes

6. Reporting Concerns

Wintellex encourages reporting of concerns related to forced labor, human trafficking, exploitation, unsafe working conditions, unethical conduct, or potential modern slavery risks.

Reports may be submitted through official Wintellex contact channels. Concerns will be reviewed based on available information and handled with appropriate confidentiality where possible.

7. Training & Awareness

Wintellex aims to promote awareness of ethical sourcing, responsible vendor management, labor rights, and modern slavery risks among relevant team members involved in procurement, vendor relationships, compliance, operations, and leadership.

8. Continuous Improvement

Wintellex recognizes that responsible business conduct requires ongoing attention. We may update our policies, vendor standards, review processes, and governance practices as our operations, partnerships, and infrastructure expand.

This statement may be updated periodically to reflect operational changes, legal developments, or improvements in our compliance and governance practices.

9. Contact

Questions regarding this Modern Slavery Statement may be directed through official Wintellex communication channels.
